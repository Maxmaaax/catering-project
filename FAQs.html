Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are the operating hours of the catering department?

Our catering department operates from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.

2. Do you offer special dietary options for students and staff?

Yes, we cater to various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal options.

3. Can I place an order for an event or departmental meeting?

Yes, you can contact our office for event catering services at least 3 days in advance.

4. How can I provide feedback or report an issue with my meal?

Your feedback is valuable. You can provide it online or visit our catering office directly.

5. Do you offer meal plans or prepaid options for regular customers?

Yes, we offer flexible meal plans for students, lecturers, and staff. Choose from weekly or monthly options.

6. Are takeaway and delivery services available?

Yes, we offer takeaway and delivery services for staff offices and student hostels.

7. Can I customize my order or make special requests?

We accommodate special requests when possible. Please inform our staff when placing your order.

8. How can I check the daily menu?

Visit our "Menu" section on the website for daily updates.

9. Do you provide catering for dietary restrictions during university events?

Yes, we cater for dietary needs like vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free during events.

10. Can external guests dine at the university's catering facilities?

Yes, we welcome visitors, including family members, to dine with us.
